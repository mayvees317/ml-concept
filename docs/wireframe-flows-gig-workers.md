## Key Design Principles

1. **Performance Intelligence**: Real-time earning optimization and market insights
2. **Income Volatility Management**: Smart cash flow smoothing and allocation
3. **Tax Optimization Focus**: Maximize deductions and minimize burden
4. **Multi-Platform Coordination**: Unified view across all gig platforms
5. **Business Growth Mindset**: From worker to entrepreneur journey
6. **Voice-First Operation**: Hands-free tracking while working
7. **Community Learning**: Peer insights and knowledge sharing
8. **Alternative Financial Services**: Credit and lending based on gig performance
9. **Long-term Wealth Building**: Beyond active work income strategies
