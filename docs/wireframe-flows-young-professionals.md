## Key Design Principles

1. **Instant Intelligence**: Value within 30 seconds of first open
2. **Progressive Disclosure**: More data = more personalized insights
3. **Anxiety Relief**: Always show reality vs perception
4. **Social Context**: Understand peer pressure and group dynamics
5. **Voice-First**: Natural language for busy professionals
6. **Achievement Focus**: Celebrate wins, build confidence
7. **Crisis Support**: Be helpful during financial stress
8. **Platform Optimization**: Fix payment app chaos step by step
