# Comprehensive Value Propositions & Product Offerings

## Executive Summary

This document outlines Money Lover's transformation from a traditional PFM app to a comprehensive Financial OS Platform serving three distinct B2C segments. Based on extensive market research, we've identified specific value propositions that address each segment's unique jobs-to-be-done, pain points, and financial behaviors. The platform leverages affiliate monetization, AI-powered insights, and omnichannel data capture to create sustainable revenue streams while solving real user problems.

**Total B2C Market Opportunity:** $31M across 35M users in SEA
**Revenue Model Transformation:** From $360K subscription ARR to $19.5M multi-stream ARR by Year 3
